Portfolio-to-value flow

Completeness before scale.

Each initiative becomes a capability work package: a decision-ready unit that can be built, governed, measured, supported, and reused.

1 · Intake

Workflow problem, consequence, owner, current artifact.

2 · Qualify

Value, feasibility, readiness, duplication, adoption burden.

3 · Bound

Human authority, fallback, escalation, source and data rules.

4 · Route

Guidance, copilot, agent, automation, low-code, model, or no build.

5 · Test

Smallest controlled intervention inside real work.

6 · Receipt

Cycle, quality, rework, exceptions, cost, risk, retained use.

7 · Disposition

Advance, revise, reuse, hold, stop, or transfer.

Gate 1 · Is the work package complete enough to build?

AdvanceReviseHold / stop
  • Named workflow owner and consequence
  • Authoritative sources and data boundary
  • Human authority, fallback, escalation
  • Measurement and support plan

Gate 2 · Did the capability earn scale?

Scale / reuseLocal onlyRetire
  • Real-work evidence, not showcase success
  • Retained use after novelty fades
  • Support and ownership after enablement
  • Reusable core separated from local variation
