Independent candidate thesis

Build AI like work that must survive the project.

A practical operating model for converting distributed M&T demand into governed, measurable, reusable delivery capacity.

AI only scales when the work package is complete: workflow, consequence, authority, evidence, owner, adoption, delivery pattern, and value receipt.

The company moment

Bechtel has formalized enterprise-wide EPC transformation leadership around advanced digital technologies, AI, automation, and robotics. M&T is growing in schedule-sensitive markets where digital delivery, repeatability, and real-time visibility matter.

The real mandate

Own the business-unit mechanism that qualifies demand, connects business workflow to architecture and governance, delivers controlled solutions, proves value, builds adoption, prevents duplication, and standardizes reusable capability.

The central contradiction

AI must move quickly enough to help active projects while remaining controlled enough to preserve accountable engineering, data governance, support ownership, and customer trust.

The candidate thesis

Treat each initiative as a capability work package—not an idea or a demo. Completeness becomes the condition for controlled build, evidence-backed scale, and cross-project reuse.

AI Capability Work Package

What must be true before an AI initiative earns scale

Work-package elementDecision it clarifiesEvidence expected
Workflow pointWhere does the intervention enter real project work?Current artifact, cadence, handoff, exception, or decision.
Business consequenceWhat delay, rework, quality risk, cost, or decision burden matters?Supportable baseline or measurement category; no invented benefit.
Human authorityWhat may AI propose or execute, and who remains accountable?Approval, escalation, override, fallback, and stop conditions.
Data and evidenceWhich sources are authoritative, permissible, current, and traceable?Source map, lineage, access, provenance, quality, and retention rules.
Owner and adoptionWho owns the workflow outcome, support, training, and correction loop?Named owner, champions, operating cadence, feedback and support path.
Delivery patternAgent, copilot, automation, low-code app, lightweight model, or no build?Architecture fit, reuse boundary, operational support, vendor role.
Value receiptWhat evidence determines advance, revise, reuse, hold, or stop?Cycle, quality, rework, retained use, exceptions, cost, or risk evidence.

Illustrative portfolio disposition

Advance

Workflow, authority, evidence, owner, and measurement are sufficiently clear for a bounded real-work build.

Revise

The problem matters, but one or more package elements need definition before development.

Reuse

An existing component, pattern, control, or training asset can meet most of the need with local adaptation.

Hold or stop

Risk, ownership, data, economics, or workflow fit does not support further investment now.
